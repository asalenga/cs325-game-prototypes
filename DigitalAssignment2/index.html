Space Bam!

  • Controls: (Move left, move right, and fire) Player 1: Left arrow key, Right arrow key, spacebar Player 2: A key, D key, 1 key
  • Originality: My game incorporates parts of a classic space shooter type game, but has a number of unique points. My game is 2-player and the enemies continuously come towards the bottom of the screen. Any time an enemy is killed, another one spawns at a random location at the top of the screen. Every time 10 enemies are killed, the speed at which they move increases.
  • Technical Merit: It incorporates 2 different sets of controls on the same keyboard.
  • Prototype Postmortem: From this prototype, I learned how to properly add in images for sprites. The easiest part was positioning the players and adding their movesets. the hardest part was adding the collision event kill enemies and lasers for when the lasers come in contact with the enemies.
